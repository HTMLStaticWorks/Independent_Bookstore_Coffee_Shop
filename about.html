About Us

A look into our story, our family, and the values we stand for at Sip & Scroll.

Our Story

Where Community, Coffee, & Literature Intersect

Sip & Scroll began in 2018 when two friends—a passionate barista and a lifelong bibliophile—realized that the perfect Saturday afternoon consisted of exactly two things: a fresh pour-over and a captivating new novel.

We believed that our local neighborhood needed a third space. A place that wasn't home, wasn't work, but a cozy sanctuary where you could lose yourself in a story or find yourself in a great conversation. Today, we source our books from independent publishers and our beans directly from fair-trade farmers in Colombia and Ethiopia.

What We Stand For

Sustainable Sourcing

Our coffee is 100% fair-trade and organic. We exclusively use compostable cups, lids, and packaging to minimize our environmental footprint.

Supporting Local Voices

We dedicate 20% of our shelf space to independent authors and local writers, ensuring diverse stories reach our community.

A Space for Everyone

Inclusivity is our core. From our accessible layout to our diverse event programming, Sip & Scroll is a safe haven for all.

Giving Back to Our Roots
